An Emmy Noether Group in Methods of AI proposal titled **"Earth Embeddings: Learning Concept Maps in Neural Nets"** has been funded by DFG. Hence, from September 2026 onwards, this group will be an Emmy Noether Group with Marc Rußwurm appointed as Junior Professor. See the [University of Bonn press release](https://www.uni-bonn.de/en/news/068-2026) for more details.
