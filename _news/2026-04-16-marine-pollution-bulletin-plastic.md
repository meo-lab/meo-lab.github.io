The paper **[Exploring plastic detectability on riverbanks using remote sensing](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.marpolbul.2026.119751)** has been accepted for publication in the *Marine Pollution Bulletin*. The paper was led by Milou Maathuis and supervised with Tim van Emmerik and Mathias Bochow.
